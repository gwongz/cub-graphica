I'm a storyteller who's expanding her toolbox. In my previous life, I worked as an online journalist, covering breaking news and features. I took a HTML and CSS class on a whim, loved it, and found my way to Hackbright Academy, a 10-week program that aims to make women awesome programmers. At Hackbright, I rapidly learned new skills, including Python and Flask, and built my first project from scratch.

I'm interested in the way technology is changing the way people connect with each other and information, and I'm excited to finally have found what I'm interested in doing - which is building usable and beautiful web applications. I'm a generalist at heart and enjoy working on products people love.
